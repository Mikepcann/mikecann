Short Term Rentals

This dataset shows all licenced Short Term Rental locations in the City of Calgary and its status. Data is provided daily by The City of Calgary.

Search By
  • Address
  • Business Licence Number
  • Licence Type
  • Type Of Residence

Secondary Suites

This dataset contains information about approved secondary suites in The City of Calgary. It can be viewed on an interactive map at http://secondarysuites.calgary.ca/ Dates for suites approved and completed prior to 2008 are set to Jan 1, 2008.

Search By
  • Address
  • Community
  • Sticker Number
  • Ward

Community Crime Stats

Data is provided monthly by the Calgary Police Service. This data is considered cumulative as late-reported incidents are often received well after an offence has occurred.

Search By
  • Category
  • Community
  • Sector
  • Year

Public Art

Information about artworks in The City of Calgary Public Art Collection, including locations, titles, artists, and descriptions of the artwork.
